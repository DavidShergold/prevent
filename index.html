Understanding Prevent

Recognizing signs of radicalization and taking action to safeguard individuals and communities.

What is Radicalization?

Radicalization is the process by which individuals develop extreme beliefs that may lead to harmful or dangerous actions. Recognizing the signs early can help protect vulnerable individuals and safeguard communities.

What is Prevent?

Prevent is a government strategy designed to safeguard individuals from radicalization. It focuses on early intervention, raising awareness, and providing support to those at risk.

Recognizing Risks

Recognizing the signs of radicalization is crucial in preventing harm and protecting individuals at risk. Early awareness allows for timely intervention, support, and education.

Signs of Radicalization

Recognizing behavioral changes early can help safeguard individuals.

Withdrawal from Family and Friends

Sudden isolation or distancing from usual relationships.

Expressing Extremist Beliefs

Strongly supporting radical ideologies or rejecting former values.

Increased Secrecy Online

Hiding social media activity or browsing extremist content.

Reporting Concerns

If you recognize signs of radicalization, report concerns through trusted channels to ensure safety.

All reports submitted through this platform are treated with the utmost confidentiality. Your privacy and security are our priority.

There is Hope

While the challenges of radicalization are real, so too is the power of awareness, compassion, and community.
